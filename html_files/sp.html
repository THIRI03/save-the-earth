SG Clean Day

Recently, SP collaborates with outside organizations for the sustainable developments.
>>SP joined Public Hygiene Council(PHC) on SG Clean Day and SP encourages students to volunteer in this program to be part of the program.

For other simple matters, SP has done pretty much in terms of environmental development.

After my studying a few weeks in SP, I noticed that the lights in the restrooms are switched on and off according to the sensor.

Not only this, the fans at the Spectrum also run for a certain interval of time and they switched off automatically. These are to control the electricity SP offers a green campus.

According to the Singapore Polytechnic official website, 230 species of flora can be found within the campus which are maintaining the green environment for the students.
